General Information

The Mighty Ducks Soccer League was established in 1996 to provide athletes residing iChicago's northside neighborhoods an environment in which to learn and play hockey. To be member of MDHL, you must be between the ages of 4 - 12 and reside in a Chicago northsidneighborhood. MDHL is run by a small full-time staff and relies on the generous volunteer timof parents and previous league members.
